FAQ

Answers to the most common questions. Click to expand.

Do I need an AI assistant?

An AI assistant can answer FAQs, capture leads, and guide users—great for support‑heavy sites. It’s optional and fully on‑brand.

Revamp vs. new website?

Revamp: upgrade what you have. New: start fresh. Both include responsive design, performance, and SEO foundations.

How long will it take?

Typical revamps: 2–6 weeks. New builds: 4–8+ weeks depending on scope. We’ll confirm a timeline after scoping.

Can I update content myself?

Yes. We can add a CMS or provide simple update guides for static sites. We also offer monthly maintenance.
